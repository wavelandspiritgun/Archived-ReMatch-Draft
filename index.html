Profile

Yo. My name is Shane. I know what it means to navigate broken systems from the inside. I didn't build this lab to be a charity; I built it to be an equalizer. I offer several services on a pay-what-you-want (or completely free, if you are in survival mode) model.

My primary engine is called Re.Match.

Society teaches you that a criminal record, addiction history, or a massive gap in your resume is a liability. Re.Match is built on the mathematical truth that those "red flags" are actually your highest-value assets.

The specific mechanism we use is called "constraints."

Standard job boards and non-profits try to fit you into boxes you don't belong in. We do the opposite. We use your exact barriers—your felony class, your supervision status, your lack of ID—as systemic filters. By doing this, we bypass the noise and unlock hyper-specific, high-tier grants, Muslim-specific programs, LGBTQ-specific resources, and second-chance employment pools that a standard college graduate couldn't access even if they tried.

This obscure corner of the internet offers you a way to take the reins. I have engineered an intake form that requires the minimal amount of information to generate maximum yield. You tell me your mandatory coordinates (demographics, record, zip code) and your current target needs. Any and all fields are optional. You only share what you want to leverage.

The current infrastructure for reentry and recovery is failing you. Here is why Re.Match exists to bypass it:

  • Data Decay: Printed resource guides and static websites go out of date the minute they are published.
  • Siloed Systems: Agencies don't talk to each other. Housing doesn't talk to Employment.
  • Hidden Requirements: Criteria are buried in bureaucratic jargon that automatically disqualifies you before you even start.
  • Volatile Funding: Specialized grants open and close without notice.
  • Algorithmic Exclusion: Corporate job boards automatically filter out people with records.

Once you submit your constraints, my system converts your profile into structured data. I deploy multiple, specialized AI agents to cross-reference your specific profile against a live database of opportunities.

Here is the critical difference: Every single time a new dossier is run, my system scans and re-verifies the entire database. It checks for dead links, expired funding, and closed programs. We don't deal in outdated binders; we deal in 99% probability, 100% live matches.

I don't compete with caseworkers; I equip you with the systemic leverage they don't have access to. Let's find your jackpot.
